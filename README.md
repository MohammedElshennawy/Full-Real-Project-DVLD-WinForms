🚗 Driving & Vehicle License Department (DVLD) System

Finally, after years of studying programming fundamentals, problem solving, OOP, databases, SQL, C#, ADO.NET, and software architecture, I reached one of the most important milestones in my learning journey: building a complete Driving & Vehicle License Department (DVLD) Management System.

This project represents the practical application of everything learned throughout the roadmap, combining software design principles, database management, and real-world business requirements into a single enterprise-style desktop application.

✨ Key Features
People & Users Management
Driver & License Management
New License Issuance Workflow
License Renewal
Replacement for Lost or Damaged Licenses
License Detention & Release
International Driving License Issuance
License Tests Management
Application Types & Test Types Management
Authentication & Permissions System
Full History Tracking & Reporting
